BUSTED



SYNOPSIS

A man named M invites seven people to a murder game party hosted by his boss, known as K. As soon as the game begins, M is shot by an unknown perpetrator, which turns the game into a real crime scene. They soon realize that the murder is related to an operation called "Project D", in which they are also involved in.

CHARACTERS
Oh Sehun
Sehun is one of the smartest of the cast. He is the one who holds the most grudges and the most competitive member of the team which is frequently seen when he was competing with the Genius Detective Team. Despite this, he is also scared of animals where he frequently calls Sejeong for help.
Kim Sejeong
She is one of the smartest members of the team and is known for her bravery yet loud screams during cases. She also has the most aegyo in the team who would always try to do it for her own or the team's benefit. She is frequently seen with Oh Sehun or Park Minyoung during cases.
Park Minyoung
Minyoung is one of the smartest of the team and the most serious one. She is often called by the other members whenever they cannot solve the code.
Yoo Jaesuk
Although it's unofficial, he mostly acts like a leader of the detective group. He is often the first to receive cases and is the one who delivers pay to the other members. However, he isn't always the fastest and is often beaten by Min-young and Jae-wook in problem-solving but he sometimes can be fast too.
Lee Kwangsoo
Lee Kwangsoo is most known for being a comedian in their group but can solve mysteries seriously as well. Kwangsoo, Jongmin, Jaewook, Jaesuk are like brothers teasing each other in some episodes. He is also known for his short temper personality.
Kim Jongmin
He is one of the main characters of Busted! and is often portrayed as the dumbest of the team.
Ahn Jaewook
He is one of the smartest because of his former occupation which he was a former agent.
